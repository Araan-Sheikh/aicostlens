## FAQ

### Do I need to connect billing accounts?

No. The MVP uses self-reported spend, seats, plans, team size, and use case. That keeps the audit fast and avoids asking for sensitive billing access.

### Will you ask for email first?

No. You see the audit first. Email is only used after the report is generated so you can save the result and receive the link.

### How are savings calculated?

Savings are calculated with deterministic rules and sourced pricing data. The AI summary does not calculate savings.

### What happens if my audit shows more than $500/month in savings?

The report highlights Credex as a strong fit for discounted AI infrastructure credits and prompts you to save the report for follow-up.

### Can I share the report publicly?

Yes. Public reports show tools, spend totals, savings, and recommendations. Email, company, role, and private lead details are not shown.
