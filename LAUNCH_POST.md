## Blog Post Draft

### Find Hidden Waste In Your AI Stack

Most startup AI spend does not begin as a budget line. It starts as one developer trying Cursor, another keeping Copilot, a founder paying for ChatGPT Team, a researcher using Claude, and an API key quietly growing behind the product. Each purchase makes sense alone. Together, the stack becomes hard to reason about.

AICostLens is a free AI spend audit for founders, CTOs, and engineering managers who want a fast second opinion. Add the tools your team pays for, the plans, monthly spend, seats, team size, and primary use case. In under two minutes, the tool shows where you may be overpaying, which seats or plans look misaligned, where tools overlap, and whether discounted AI infrastructure credits could help.

The important part: the audit math is deterministic. AI does not invent savings. Pricing assumptions are documented in `PRICING_DATA.md`, and the LLM is only used to summarize the already-calculated result. If there is not much to save, AICostLens says so. If there is a meaningful savings opportunity, the report explains why.

For high-savings cases, AICostLens points users toward Credex, which helps companies capture discounted AI infrastructure credits without forcing the team to rip out tools that are working.

Try it here: https://aicostlens.shop/
